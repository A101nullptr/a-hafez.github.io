    The design incorporated mainly charcoal tiles, cream beige ceramic tiles and white paint with the addition of dark wooden vanity cabinets and wooden plank shelves in order to give a sense of contrast from the surrounding light colors. A black and white tribal rug was placed as a subtle pattern to the area while maintaining its sleek clean design.
