    | Concept Explanatory Diagrams |

    The Journey | Visitors enter through a series of colourful drapes, from attraction to beauty, alluring them to continue on and solve the puzzle presented to them in the form of colour coded fragments of photographs on tulle fabric that is in its nature, strong yet elegant and refined; as such those pin-ups were.

    Passing by rather than passing through, the path of optical illusion, guides them to the last part of the exhibition, a black and white display of all of those stars, rid of all of the glamour and colours, to a raw exhibit of their authenticity, Such as that the path of those nameless stars, the shining lights of fame, glamorous and grace, promising a bright future, to be ended abruptly, almost there but never quite.
